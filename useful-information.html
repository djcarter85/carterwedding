Useful Information

Rejoice in the Lord always. I will say it again: Rejoice!

Philippians 4:4

The ceremony will take place at St Andrew the Great Church, St Andrew's Street, Cambridge, CB2 3AX.

The reception will take place at Jesus College, Cambridge, CB5 8BL.

There is plenty of parking available at Jesus College. It is free on the day, but there is a small charge to park overnight. Please indicate on the RSVP form if you would like to park here, and for how long.

There are many hotels available in Cambridge of varying prices. There is also the option of staying at Jesus College. You will need to use the code MS310721 to view availability and to book.

Dress code is formal (not morning suits), but do feel free to wear whatever makes you comfortable, particularly shoes where we recommend at least a comfy pair to change into. The evening is more relaxed and there will be dancing so wear what you would like.
